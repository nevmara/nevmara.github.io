## Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Our published applications may vary based on the features they offer. Our data collection and processing practices are categorized below according to the type of application you are using:

### 1. Applications with Advertising and Analytics
To support the development of our free applications and improve user experience, we may use third-party Service Providers such as **Google AdMob**, **Unity Ads**, and **Unity Analytics**.

If the application you are using contains advertisements or analytics infrastructure, these services may automatically collect the following data in the background:
* **Device or other IDs:** Identifiers such as your device's Android Advertising ID (AAID).
* **Usage Data:** Your IP address, device model, operating system version, and in-app interaction information.

**Purpose of Use:** This data is collected and shared solely for the purposes of personalizing advertisements for you, measuring application performance, and preventing fraud/abuse. The source code of our applications does not manually request personal data from you, such as your name, location, or contacts.

You can access the privacy policies of the third-party service providers we use below:
* [Google Play Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* [Google AdMob](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)
* [Unity Analytics and Unity Ads](https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy)

### 2. Applications Requiring Membership and Accounts
If the application you are using requires you to create a profile or log in (e.g., email address, username), this personal data you provide is collected and processed solely for the purposes of managing your account, saving your in-app progress, and providing the service to you.

### 3. Offline and Ad-Free Applications
Our applications that do not contain any advertising networks, analytics tools, or membership requirements (e.g., some of our fully local puzzle or utility apps) **do not collect, process, or transmit** any personal data or device IDs from your device.
